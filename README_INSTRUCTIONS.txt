The blocks are able to be dragged to be put on top of one another. The camera can also rotate, zoom, and pan to look around the environment. To move a block, click and drag the cursor on it towards another position. In order to use the camera, press down the Q key and use the mouse to control the camera. To rotate, use the left-click and drag; to zoom, use the scroll-wheel and drag; to pan, use the right-click and drag.
